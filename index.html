About Us

SkyForge Aerospace is a forward-thinking startup dedicated to revolutionizing the aerospace industry. Founded in 2023, we aim to bridge the gap between Earth and the cosmos with cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices. Our team of engineers and scientists is committed to pushing the boundaries of space exploration.

Our Vision

At SkyForge Aerospace, we envision a future where space travel is accessible, sustainable, and beneficial to all. We are developing innovative propulsion systems and eco-friendly spacecraft to reduce the carbon footprint of space missions, ensuring a cleaner universe for generations to come.

Our Technology

Our flagship technologies include the NovaDrive propulsion system, which offers 30% greater efficiency than traditional engines, and the EcoSat platform, a modular satellite designed for minimal environmental impact. These innovations are set to launch in 2026, marking a new era in aerospace engineering.

Our Achievements

In 2024, SkyForge Aerospace successfully completed its first suborbital test flight, reaching an altitude of 120 kilometers. We also secured a $5 million grant from the Global Space Initiative to fund our sustainable satellite program, positioning us as a leader in green aerospace technology.
